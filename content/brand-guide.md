## Typography
- **Headings:** Inter (bold/extrabold), tight tracking
- **Body:** Inter (regular), relaxed leading
- **Code:** JetBrains Mono or system monospace
- **Size scale:** clamp-based for responsive (e.g., `clamp(1.8rem, 4vw, 2.4rem)`)

## Logo

### The Mark
A brain/cloud silhouette with a lightning bolt ‚Äî represents AI (brain) gaining persistent memory (lightning = awakening). The warm orange gradient conveys energy without aggression.

### Usage Rules
- Always use on dark background or with sufficient contrast
- Minimum size: 32x32px for the icon, 120px wide for logo + text
- Don't rotate, stretch, or recolor
- The lightning bolt is the signature element ‚Äî it should always be visible
